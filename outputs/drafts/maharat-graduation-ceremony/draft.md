# Maharat Holds Graduation Ceremony for March 2022 Intake

**Maharat Construction Training Center marked the completion of its twelfth trainee cohort in a graduation ceremony held at the Technical and Vocational Training Corporation campus in Dammam on March 9, 2023.**

On March 9, 2023, Maharat Construction Training Center held a graduation ceremony for its twelfth trainee cohort at Ghazi Al Gosaibi Hall, located on the campus of the Technical and Vocational Training Corporation in Dammam.

The ceremony recognised the achievement of trainees who completed their programs as part of the March 2022 intake. Maharat's graduation ceremonies have consistently brought together trainees, their families, and representatives from sponsoring industry partners — reflecting the collaborative model that underpins the center's approach to construction workforce development.

The event is consistent with Maharat's broader mandate to develop qualified Saudi nationals for the construction sector, in alignment with the Saudization objectives of Saudi Vision 2030. Graduation ceremonies at Maharat serve not only as a formal recognition of individual achievement but also as an occasion to acknowledge the contributions of sponsoring companies and the institutional support that enables trainees to complete their programs.

Maharat Construction Training Center continues to deliver structured diploma programs across key construction disciplines, equipping graduates with the technical competencies required by the industry. The center's ongoing graduation cycles reflect a sustained pipeline of nationally trained construction professionals entering the workforce.
