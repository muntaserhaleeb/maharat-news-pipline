# Maharat and Samsung E&A Sign Training Agreement for 300 Saudi Trainees

**Maharat Construction Training Center signed a training sponsorship agreement with Samsung E&A on February 16, 2026, enrolling 300 Saudi trainees in one-year diploma programs set to begin in April 2026.**

Maharat Construction Training Center (Maharat) signed a training agreement with Samsung E&A on February 16, 2026, under which Samsung E&A will sponsor 300 Saudi trainees enrolled in Maharat's one-year diploma programs. Training is scheduled to commence in April 2026.

The agreement was announced during Maharat's First Female Graduation Ceremony, held under the patronage of Eng. Mansour Al-Shehri, Chairman of the Board of Trustees, and attended by members of the Maharat Board of Trustees alongside dignitaries from the Saudi Contractors Authority, the Human Resources Development Fund, and Saudi Aramco.

Under the terms of the agreement, trainees will gain practical skills and hands-on experience through Maharat's structured diploma curriculum. The partnership reflects an intent to expand employment pathways for national talent and to strengthen the contribution of the private sector to construction workforce development in the Kingdom.

The agreement aligns with Saudi Vision 2030 objectives for economic diversification and human capital development. By linking training delivery directly to industry sponsorship, the arrangement is designed to ensure that graduates enter the workforce with competencies that meet sector demand.

The signing forms part of a broader set of agreements concluded by Maharat on the same occasion, which also included partnerships with the Saudi Contractors Authority Academy, Halumm Construction Innovation Company, TÜV SÜD, and NEFT Energies — reflecting Maharat's ongoing efforts to deepen industry engagement and expand training opportunities for Saudi talent across the construction sector.
